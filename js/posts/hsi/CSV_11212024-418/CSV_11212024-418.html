UNDUP - Total 12-month unduplicated headcount
12-month unduplicated headcount: 2022-23 Indicates how many individuals - all students (EFFYALEV=1,EFYTOTLT) the institution served over a 12-month period (the unduplicated headcount) Unduplicated count - The sum of students enrolled for credit with each student counted only once during the reporting period, regardless of when the student enrolled. Credit - Recognition of attendance or performance in an instructional activity (course or program) that can be applied by a recipient toward the requirements for a degree, diploma, certificate, or other formal award.

PCTE12HS - Percent of 12-month unduplicated headcount that are Hispanic/Latino
Percent of 12-month unduplicated headcount that are Hispanic/Latino. This variable is derived from the 12-month (July 1 - June 30) enrollment component that is collected in the fall surveys. Hispanic - A person of Mexican, Puerto Rican, Cuban, Central or South American or other Spanish culture or origin, regardless of race. This variable is derived by dividing total Hispanic/Latino unduplicated headcount (EFFYALEV=1,EFYHISPT) by the grand total unduplicated headcount (EFFYALEV=1,EFYTOTLT). Ratios are converted to percentages by multiplying by 100 and then are rounded to whole numbers.
